Brightward Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 16, 2026

Brightward is a local-first app with no servers of its own. Our principle is simple: your data belongs to you and stays on your device by default.

What we store, and where

Your course progress, practice records, journal entries, mood check-ins, sunshine and achievements, and app settings are stored only in your device's local storage. Brightward has no accounts and no backend — we cannot view, collect, or back up any of your content.

Cloud AI (optional, with your explicit consent)

The "Reframe" feature can optionally use cloud AI. Only after you explicitly consent the first time you use it, the text you type for that request is sent over an encrypted connection to the third-party AI service Volcano Engine Ark (privacy policy: https://www.volcengine.com/trust/privacy) solely to generate a response. Nothing that identifies you is attached, and the app keeps none of this text anywhere off your device. You can decline cloud AI entirely — the app falls back to built-in guidance, and everything else works normally.

Voice journaling

Speech is transcribed entirely on your device (using Apple's speech recognition). Neither audio nor transcripts ever leave the device.

Notifications and alarms

Journal reminders and the happiness alarm are local notifications / system alarms. No server is involved.

Subscriptions and payments

Subscriptions are handled entirely by Apple's App Store. We never touch or store any of your payment information.

What we don't do

No ads, no third-party analytics SDKs, no tracking, no selling of data, no device identifiers collected.

Deleting your data

Me → Clear All Data erases everything stored locally in one tap. Deleting the app removes all data as well.

Children

Brightward is not designed for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children (in fact, we collect no one's personal information).

Important note

Brightward's content is for learning and personal growth. It is not medical or psychological advice and is not a substitute for professional help. If you are in crisis, please contact your local professional helpline or emergency services immediately.

Changes to this policy

Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
